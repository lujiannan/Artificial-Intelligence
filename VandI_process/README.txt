Info:
The files included in this directory is used to do correction to the source images

Tools:
Pycharm

Instruction:
-image_slice.py: slice certain images
-cvs2labelme.py: convert from cvs format to what labelme could read
-labelme_json_dataread.py: reference download from the internet
-video_slice.py: slice a video on its size
-video2image.py: convert specific video into frames, frequency of frame-snipping could be changed.
